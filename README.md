## **ğŸ•¹ï¸ How to Play**

1. **Setup:** Choose player count, assign special roles, and optionally enter names.  
2. **Pass & Reveal:** Pass the device. Each player taps to secretly see their identity and word.  
3. **Discuss:** Everyone takes turns saying *one word* to describe their card.  
4. **Vote:** The group discusses (before the timer runs out\!) and votes to eliminate the most suspicious player.  
5. **Win Conditions:**  
   * **Civilians:** Eliminate all Undercovers and Mr. Whites.  
   * **Impostors:** Survive until you outnumber the Civilians.  
   * **Jester:** Get voted out by the group.  
   * **Mr. White:** Guess the civilian word correctly upon elimination.
